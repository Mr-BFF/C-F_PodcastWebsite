New Associate Fish is Arriving as Main Staff for Next Year

By AngeloJacksonED - August 12th, 2021.



Fishy, a successful Blogger and Business Owner joins Frog Does It All!

So many updates!!!!

Hello. Series 3 is almost getting ready and we are pleased to share with you a new member of our staff. Fish aka ItsFishy13, to be more specific (and is also a real name) will be joining us when our older assistant will be leaving the Podcast to head towards College life. Our older assistant was a staff member before, doing full time duties in ED14Entertainment. His decision to leave doesn’t go without leaving a smile in everyone’s faces. He will be leaving when Series 3 comes to a close.

In the meanwhile, Fish is running a blog with Brice named robotandfish.com which was a WordPress blog that our cast member Robot 2 will be participating in. That site get’s update regularly. Fish is also a small business owner of Fish’s Production Service might be listed on Google Maps very soon. Fish’s Production Services is a place where people can get expert advice from Fish about Web Design, App Development, Photography and Video Editing! Andrew is a one of the managers of the Business as well! While we have no plans to acquire the business fully, instead Fish was hired into EDEntertainment14 for his expertise in blogging, typing and writing. Though he may be featured in an Episode for Frog Does It All, he will be currently writing Blog Posts for us. This was made by careful considerations that took place.

This change once spoken by Angelo said that it was a smart move, since he’s been writing 70% of the Podcast’s articles. He is also responsible for 90% of the Guides and Articles since he can’t double the project on. He’s also been doing real life work as well. Angelo was responsible for the site’s blogs and dong the work into updating it.

Andrew, the Senior Staff member had to say this within the change: “As we head back into full-time work, we need our staff members to be focused, energetic and ready for what’s coming ahead. We’ll be setting a schedule of when an episode gets released once a month without failure. The research group will active and will be seeing what types of techniques for the podcast works or not.”

Fish will begin to write articles for us, making his debut and will replace our old staff member on 2022. Fish responding to his entrance in ED14Entertainment and the Frog Does It All! Podcast, “Greatly honored to work with Andrew and the rest of the staff on the Podcast. I was intrigued on how things work out for the staff and how their website is booming. I am looking forward to working on this amazing project next year and will start to write for this year!

Also, Layla is stepping down for a while as Main host, but don’t worry, she’ll be back! That leaves Robot Jr as Sole Host of the Podcast. The Question of The Day will still occur and so will our website updates coming soon! As August comes by into a schedule that will be unknown for us, Frog Does It All will return in Late September. New episodes are already in production as we go ahead into the new series, production is key here because when we go back to work full-time, there will be no time to balance the boat and determine what to work on in the afternoon and evening. Stay tuned everyone! Oh, and a return for Camel, will be taking into consideration as well.

What can YOU do? Feel free to join our Discord community! We love to see you there, we can talk about your website or podcast and we’ll give you feedback! Also check our robotandfish.com and support Fish’s Production Services coming soon! Stay tuned for our new Q&A update on this website as well as new articles coming your way!

Thanks once again and see you for another article or for Series 3 starting in late September!
