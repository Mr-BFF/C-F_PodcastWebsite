The Green Butterfly Plushy is Found

By Andrew Drive - September 11th, 2021.



Here's the Green Butterfly.

Short Article Highlight. Camel was right!

Hello. This is a quick article. For updates, check out or Twitter or visit our Observatory on Discord! Andrew Drive’s Observatory

Our clues have been found. Camel’s to be specific. The Green Butterfly is discovered. We mentioned the whereabouts of Green Butterfly from Episode 6 from Series 2. Camel said: “It’s the mystery of the green butterfly. I do not remember seeing a green butterfly, but a person who spoke to the podcast said there was a green butterfly before in the Vancouver Convention Center”. “Is it real?” Robot 2 said. “The person thinks it is real. The one that is featured is in the color Orange. I see. It went missing and one theory is that it was given away, possibly to another parent’s children."

Until then, we never knew if it was real. On September 10th, Recently, we found an image of Green Butterfly which was found in Mr B.F.F’s Google Photos profile. Apparently, he was just checking his phone and the gallery online. That was in December, obviously. 2015 (photo labeled as 2015) or 2013 (date of the photo taken, probably). When it was found, it was then sent to the Podcast and finally, it was also posted to Twitter. Andrew said this: “What an interesting discovery!” For now, we will note this down for a future episode which is great to know!

Alright. That’s all for now. We can confirm that Camel and our Friend was right! Stay tuned for the Series 3 premiere on September 26th 2021! Hope you all have a great rest of your day!
