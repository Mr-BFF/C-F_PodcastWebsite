New Cover, New Updates, and New Episodes!

By AngeloJacksonED - Feb 25th, 2021.


Hello guys! Angelo here! We have stunning and great updates for you to look at!

To start: We have these updates.

A New Cover! What do you think of it! You may use our covers to promote our podcast via the branding page. Don't modify the photo though!

A Transcripts Page is linked to our site! Here our podcast fans will be able to access our podcast transcripts to any available episode. However some episode won't have transcripts made by us because our main focus is to work on more episodes of our podcast. Whilst we work on more episodes, you may help us with transcripts! If you like to help us out and make transcripts for the site, download a copy of our episode and analyze our episode then begin writting. Audio to Text providers are also allowed and good to use. Contact us once after you made a copy to send us so we can put it on the website! We will credit you with your name if applicable. Thanks for contributing to our podcast resources!

Third, we have added a new video to our gallery, Robot on Chain Reaction Intro Parody! Hmm. We wonder why Robot is featured on our website....

So far, with that in mind, we have prepped a script for Episode 6 of our podcast! We are on the works right now, so we won't spoil anything so it's really much up to you to keep track of updates and new episodes coming. We are on multiple Streaming platforms so be sure to hit subscribe or follow on any cover you see where we distribute our podcast.

We are also thinking on having merch on for our podcast. We have to prioritize things first before we can get into that. We have set up a Spring account for later though. Stay tuned if your interested in seeing that.

And finally... we use Audry.io to grow our podcast. Although it may not live up to our standards, it is truely navigational and soon we will be using it even more to grow our podcast. To see for yourself, here is our profile!

Also we have content collabs, while we do caller collabs, we can still do podcast collabs and talk about anything we want, (eg. Interviews, content co-creation).

Be sure to check us out on Audry.io if you want. We might be doing more forms of promotion in the near future, but for now, more episodes are to be produced.

Thank you for taking the time to read this, and have fun, enjoy our website and our podcast!
