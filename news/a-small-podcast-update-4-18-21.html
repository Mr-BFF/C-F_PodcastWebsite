A Small Podcast Update: 04/18/2021

By camelfrogadmin - April 18th, 2021.


What's happening? Another episode, another save? What will it be?

A banner named "Updates!"

Hello. Did you watch Episode 8? We hope you did. Make sure to check it out. Our friend Mr B.F.F finds it real funny, and he was definitely laughing so hard! Have a good look at it. Available to where you listen to your favorite podcasts. For now, let's talk about plans and updates that will go in and for some that will needs to be in by a timely manner.

UPDATE ON CAMEL:

Anyways, let's sort things out for our community to learn..... Camel is not yet fixed and his fluff is soon getting worse and is detaching. We are awaiting more news regarding the fix and Andrew has brought up a person who will be able to fix it. The others he called aren't that interested in the prospect of getting him fixed because they say, we're busy! It's saddening to see the news and the wait will have to take a little longer for now. Although making episodes and staying active on the internet is most important for us, although minor flaws can happen. We mostly do care about Camel even more. Stay tuned for more updates regarding Camel! Hopefully things will get fixed here as soon as possible!

EPISODES IN PRODUCTION:

What's been happening with the latest episodes? We have already prepared some of the script, it's almost complete. We have kept in touch with Robot Jr. In the last episode, it had a minor flaw of asking Butterfly, How's Robot 2 doing? It was a unforgettable mistake and we take it seriously when it comes to our episode standards. This time, the final script of episode 9 will be reviewed especially when Camel gets fixed, some parts may be altered. Yet the episode still shows good ratings. So for the viewers who went on the poll or were predicting at the time, unfortunately, we can guarantee that Camel will not be fixed before Episode 9 gets released. So it could be the chance that viewers can get it right if Camel gets fixed just when Episode 9 gets released. The crew will start early as soon as possible so we can get things done and it's over with....

Episode 10 plans are on the way as Macaroni will be in contact when we begin filming but it will also be the day of the promotion swap. Our friends have been in touch with us and is planning to have our trailer to be released on May 8th! That's going to be 2 weeks prior! Yet, Episode 9 is almost finish so we are in the brinks of a last minute production by the time Episode 9 gets released in order to gain more trust and respect! We plan to air our friend's promotion on the same date or on May 9th. According to this post seen here.....

An image of the message regarding the promotion swap.

Yet we are posting more episodes this month and that's a good thing. However in this ultimate rush so far, Angelo contacted Mr B.F.F to see if he could help for the final time and have him left alone.... but however, he's on a large and super hard week to get a long 5 day break. Mr B.F.F is nervous for this week but he'd agreed to spend some time once more because he is definitely long time friends, and he'll always have our backs. according to Angelo. Angelo is wishing best of luck to Mr B.F.F in hopes of coming back and doing some of the work for us. We believe in him that he'll do good.
