A New Format - New Cast + Updates!

By AngeloJacksonED - June 1st, 2021.



A New Format that Significantly Changes the Podcast!

Want a Full Size Wallpaper? Let Us Know!

Hello everyone. As everyone knows, at least new episode gets released every month. As production is finished and June is now starting, episode 11 is set to be released on June 12th.

What’s today’s story?

We have built a second version of the Camel and Frog Podcast. As you might have known, Our Podcast name has changed to “Frog Does It All”. Here, we have a new format and a new set of people who will come in and give their opinions as we progress into our new Season that we will plan for September.

Meet the new cast!

Robot 2

Robot 2 is a former parody actor, whose roles was playing Millionaire and Guest Caller for the Camel and Frog Live Podcast twice!

Robot Jr

Robot Jr was a guest caller for Episode 9 of the podcast. Robot Jr will no longer be part of the podcast and is choosing to retire and do the opinion giver role until he leaves.

Layla will be the main host. And Finally, We have Frog.

Our podcast is going through major changes….. These will be shown through out the first release of Episode 11. After Camel’s leave, things took a wild change. Therefore, there is now a second version of “Frog Does It All” after two episodes started the changes quite nicely.

Here are the changes....

1. Frog is no longer the sole host of the podcast. This podcast isn’t going alone. Aside are three members who will assist him with the callers along the journey as long as Camel is able to preside again to the podcast. No word on the update yet.

2. The format has changed. The host (Layla) will be narrating and leading where the next part, the start or the end goes to. Meaning that Frog will no longer narrate along for pretty much a long time. He will be talking with the caller and sometimes may be asking you folks at home something at some points! He will still be the guy who will provide topics for most activities or entertainment stories.

3. After each caller leaves, Robot 2 will be interviewing the callers after Frog finishes the story. Robot 2 will be the announcer and the interviewer. These segments may take up to 10 seconds to 2 minutes long if possible.

4. Robot Jr will take place as a opinion giver. He will give his opinions and Layla will give Frog’s Question of The Day. Robot Jr will respond to it and then you can give your answers as well!

With these changes and a much needed challenge for the Podcast, it will be more entertaining and wide open as possible as much as we can say, if we have the time to provide the episodes, our doors will be open to more viewers as we keep growing and making new ideas. Get your seat-belts fasten and we will get this show moving! The new episode will take place on June 12th on Saturday. Stay Tuned!

UPDATES ON OUR MOBILE APP:

Last article, we told you how we released our new app. We have made and build updates to our app and now, we have fully released our app for production! We have built some mild changes to it and hopefully you enjoy it. Even though, it may seem stressful for us, it just had to be a checklist full of challenges on and off with the Podcast.

Conclusion:

For now, we are focusing on life stuff right now and the next 3 weeks will push us to the end. Wonder if we are going to make it to the Finale and open our doors to a much needed Summer break? Come back here for updates! For now check out the Listen Page on June 12th for the Episode 11 release. Well, have a great day!

- The Frog Does It All Team.
