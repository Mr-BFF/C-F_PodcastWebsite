ALL NEW EPISODES ARE IN PRODUCTION FOR SERIES 3

By Fishy Cohen - August 22nd, 2021.



ALL NEW EPISODES ARE IN PRODUCTION FOR SERIES 3

Tune in on Late September for Series 3!

Welcome Back! Recently, we are going back into work for Frog Does It All’s new Season and as you might have seen, new changes are now implemented into the Website to match the new updates coming ahead as well as a new Questions Archive and an Updated About Page. Stay tuned for more Website Updates!

Alright, we are now returning to business as Staff is partially resuming in session for what it will be an unexpected s chedule in Late August. The plan is if we don’t have time to work on this in Early September, making the episodes now is a great benefit to the podcast. Just standing there won’t help us at all and can put us at a disadvantage since we are bound to work Full Time soon.

So far, a new episode and trailer has been made for the new Series that will have Robot 2, Robot Jr and Frog. There will be news for Camel appearing every once a while in the episodes. Layla has stepped down and will partially return for the new Series. The episodes may have 1 or two new Callers. Our Android App will also be updated so we can share the Episodes there as well. More episodes are in production and the Senior staff has been working lately so it’s all up to them to evaluate what’s going to be done for the day and what episode scripts and notebooks can be sorted out.

The goal of Series 3 is that length will be much longer and more unique content. Each episode will be labeled with 1x13. 1 meaning the First Episode in the Series and 13 meaning the latest episode in all Series. 2x14 would be the next in the list.

While we are not going to share more specifics and stuff here, follow us today because the Podcast is returning on Late September. Make sure to stay tuned for all new episodes!!!! Senior Staff member, Angelo had something to say for the new Series ahead that This will be a much better improved and longer Series as well promised. This will have us more devoted to the Podcast and I can’t wait for my Team to work on it.

In the meanwhile, our website is going into expiration in 4 months and we need your help! DONATE TODAY via our Ko-Fi or our Developer Andrew Drive today! We already have the first $10 covered. So we only need $3 since our price is set to $12.70 for the renewal costs. We will also be working on getting it renewed too as well. We a new budget strategy here and we are not here to fail.

We recently opened up a community for you on Discord where you can share opinions, websites and Podcasts there! If you share your Podcast, we may just shout you out on a Future Episode. Join us as well on the Andrew Drive’s Observatory.
