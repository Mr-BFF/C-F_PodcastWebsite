Robot 2 is getting his own site: Robot2Guides

By AngeloJacksonED - July 19th, 2021.



robot2guides.net is a Side Project for the C-F Podcast!

A banner showing off Robot2Guides coming soon.

As Series 2 comes to an end, the staff is taking a break while others are planning to build more projects and other stuff for EDTeam14. When Episode 12 got release the crew began creating ideas and the side project we promised to everyone is Robot2Guides, it started as a YouTube channel. We wanted to add more places for Robot2Guides to be seen so we are building a world wide website yet again.

It will contain Robot 2. Robot 2, your's truly is a Studio Reporter for Frog Does It All! He has appeared as a former parody actor and appeared in two episodes of the Camel and Frog Live Podcast era! He is accompanied by his friendly Robot Jr.

Assistant Mr B.F.F joined in to do two Tech tutorials on the Fluid DVD player which would come out soon, Angelo is working on a bunch of blog posts during the day while his friend wants to use the computer and make a long video. As of right now, the site is almost done, we need the domain to work and build the last touches of our website, so stay tuned for that. Angelo just loved building ideas even day-dreaming in bed that it will work out successfully. We will see how this plays out. Andrew Drive will also be joining in to assist Angelo and some staff members from EDTeam14 will monitor the site too.

The full story will be on the main website on Robot2Guides.net as soon as it comes out.

More updates for side-projects to come soon! In the meanwhile, we've got community updates for you.

First and foremost, we would like to thank everybody who has been working on the Podcast and the website. We could have not done it without you. Secondly, our website will be getting it's upgrades ASAP. Many people asked us to fix the Home Page due to the lack of speed it can load. It has something to do with the Cookie Banner we've made and we'll take a look at it.

Thirdly, EDTeam14.com might be closed down early since we didn't buy the correct plan and now we have to close down our site. Meaning we'll have to drop our Main Website and have our LinkedIn page do all the work since it's easy to use and it's simple. Fourth Update, we have already made a new script for the premiere and will be building another one after August ends. However, it's still summer and we are doing something else.

We hope that you have a good rest of your day!
