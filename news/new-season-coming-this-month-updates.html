New Season Begins September 26th! + Updates

By Andrew Drive - September 7th, 2021.



Series 3 begins Soon......

Coming soon on September 26th!

Hello. It’s coming. SEPTEMBER 26th, 2021. The New Season of Frog Does It All / Camel and Frog Live Podcast! Begins. 2 Episodes are already set for their scheduled release. The Goal, 1 episode every month, longer sessions and possibly, a bigger audience than before. Please make sure to follow us and get notified of when new episodes roll out so you don’t miss anything! Also check out our streaming services to see the episodes go live in action as well. If you are unfamiliar with the stories made so far, please check our other blog posts, they’ll kindly get you up to speed with what’s happening.

Basically, in short - Staff took careful considerations with work, and a bunch of paperwork we had to submit. We ultimately made the decision to release Episode 1 on September 26. Make sure to stay tuned for more updates! Right now, Episode 2 & 3 is in works for it’s release. Stay tuned everyone! We will also have exciting new updates to share in our Twitter and our Discord server Andrew Drive’s Observatory, so stay tuned for those! Hope the halfway point of 2021 is going to be a successful one! We are ALSO excited too!

We also got some great news, as this season makes waves, we now have full control of our Domain after our older owner bought it his way. We are going to extend the domain for 1 additional year! This can help us maintain our website and we can get more visitors here without the stress and the hard stuff. We also got our own Business listing dedicated named: The Camel and Frog Podcast Productions recently. We will accept messages from our viewers, you can see what our friends put, what services we provide, the photos we can share with our viewers and will sometimes open the reviews. Feel free to check us out! We will also make some posts there as well! We will look for more ways to grow our Podcast and Website without paying for services eventually.

In the meanwhile, the staff will also be going back to work in real life too for hours. This means that staff is on schedule of when we can eventually work on our Podcast. With Angelo who is our Main leader of the Podcast, is away for days recently, Andrew is taking over since we called him Owner of the Podcast to do the job. Angelo will continue to work on Robot2Guides which we will eventually collab with since we got guides rolling with them. For now, Angelo is just going to be away for a while. He’ll come back later in the Season ahead so as Layla, the Main host as well.

Please let us know what you think? Are you hyped already for our new Season, we’ll be ready to post ahead! We’ll be doing some mild changes to our site too, so if there is downtime, please be patient and we’ll let you know if somethings wrong on Twitter too. We will also move some clips over to our new YouTube channel as well. Stay tuned for that.

For now, we’ll just be busy! We’ll talk later and we’ll see you there! Until then.
