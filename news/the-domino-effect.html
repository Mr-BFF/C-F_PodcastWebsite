Activities: The Domino Effect

By Mr B.F.F - March 8th, 2021.


Flashback time! We are going back when we had lots of toys that we played long before the pandemic!

Today, we look back at the toys we played before. We have gotten into Dominoes a little while so we used Jenga bricks to emulate them as Dominoes! We are talking about some form of bricks that are set up in which it soon gets toppled down and then sets off a chain reaction if executed. We recommend that you search on YouTube "dominoes" if you like to see some inspiration and more background of what it is. Each of our clips we made at the time were recorded on a 2DS for fun.

Our version of Dominoes toppling down is a bit different from the others, here's why.

1. We have full standards of getting everything perfect. No miss is possible and should be. Outtakes are kept for the archive.

2. Each ball goes into a unique goal net after the dominoes sets off.

3. At the end, this is where both Camel and Frog shows up! That's right, this is one of the reasons why we are making this article. Our friend had the idea of messing up the stage after the dominoes fall off.

How was the idea made? When the ball goes into the goal post, it triggers our friend to throw the Camel and Frog into the rink as a celebration. It would soon became a event where this happened everytime when new stages are set up to film. DISCLAMER: The puppets are not harmed in the recordings, they are just fine!

This took place in December 2018 until February 2019 before we went into other stuff.

We are looking forward to doing this again, however our Jenga game has been stored and one other set we had was sold away.

Angelo went to comment and said, it's something productive to do, I don't see anything bad about this. I think he had lots of fun.

Attached will be some clips for you to see!
