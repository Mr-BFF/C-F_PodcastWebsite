Some August Updates and What We've Been Doing

By AngeloJacksonED - August 2nd, 2021.



Quick August Update and What We've Done During our Break!

The 3 Main Characters in the Podcast!

Hello, happy August everyone! With just 1 month left before the kids return to school, things start to gradually resume again, and for us, we will return in late September for Series 3 of Frog Does It All. Lately, we haven’t been updating our website here since the Season Finale has been released but however, we will be changing the navigation of our website still. We have been spending time publishing Robot2Guides and Repairs very often and we produced our website, Robot2Guides.xyz. In there, we have built 6 new guides and 6 new blog posts, if you have time, please have a look at them. We at the EDEntertainment Productions co, would like to present this to our community. Not only, we do recreational tutorials, we do tech, business, gaming and website tutorials! We also having been saving a small amount of money so we can boost our projects to pro. You can also donate to us so you can help us support the projects.

What have we’ve been doing? We’ve been relaxing, going outside and sometimes we have been preparing new scripts for the next season of our podcast, we can’t wait to share them. However, while the weather is still at it and that summer break is still here and ending in 1 month, our good friend of ours has only one year of school left before he goes to college, we’ve been making less plans than usually, but to work ahead for life. When our friend reaches College, things are going to be a lot different for him and for us when we needed him the most for our Podcast. He’s still in High School returning full time and will be working harder than the last time things had changed. He has devoted time for us in the Frog Does It All podcast side of things during days he’s not in duty and when he finished homework and now he’ll be leaving but never, without supporting us and continue to listen, that way he can interact in the City and connect with other people and businesses in his Area. Also for us, we do have real jobs and we love doing them. While staff may be still closed here, we do have a schedule of what’s gonna happen in the near future. We are wishing him nothing but the best as soon as he leaves in the upcoming 2022 year.

Aside from that note for now, while we are off, we’ve been playing a bunch of games and we wanted to try out a loyalty app for playing games to earn a few bucks. We tried it with an $.50 Amazon gift card and it worked. So now we can earn some side bucks if we can which will also help us with some work and projects directly as well. You can view the guide on Robot2Guides’ website. We’ll be working on more guides for Robot2Guides for now and we won’t change the site’s look just yet. Maybe in mid-August, those will be rolled out. In the meanwhile, you can read full stories on the site so you know what’s been going on there recently. We’ll continue to write here as well.

For now, no new episode. We are just chilling for now and doing work at the moment and we’ll get back into it very soon.

We hope you are staying safe and have a great rest of your day! Another article soon!

From the Frog Does It All Team.
