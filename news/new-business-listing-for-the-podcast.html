New Business Prop - Ending of August 2021 Updates.

By Andrew Drive - August 29th, 2021.



The Business is now coming to the Search Engine.

Some updates going into September!

Hello there, Andrew here and I’ve been busy ever since. Here’s some highlights from the Camel and Frog Podcast Team. Join us on our Discord for other updates on the Andrew Drive’s Observatory.

The Camel and Frog Productions is now a Business for filming Photography and Video. This calls after we needed a bunch of pictures for our short videos. We also have done the editing as well. We used to film Camel and Frog in parody videos and photos, as you may have noticed through out our website and our Podcast. We can say it’s original for sure. We won’t be able to serve in person for now but we can still show you interactives through our website! In the Business Profile, we will have links, merch, services and posts directly on the listing. (note that we are not permanently closed, it’s just there for our own reasons) More stuff will be in our Business Profile.

Shout-out to Robot2Guides (our, Angelo’s companion project) who helped us create the Business listing. The name will be changed to point Camel and Frog Podcast. Make sure to go check out Robot2Guides as well! We will also be partnering with Robot2Guides to help you build pictures and videos for your portfolio soon! Right now, the company is more focused into Tech at the moment.

In other news, We are turning more into some kind of business to help us grow. There were 200 views in the first round and there was plenty of potential. No signs of other traffic coming in at the moment. But you can help us grow by visiting our listing. Ratings doesn’t seem to work at the moment. We’ll let you know when you can make a review for us!

We will also be releasing a new round of Merch! We will have some new line of clothing for sure, wallpapers, audio you can buy, and other round of digital or physical products for your enjoyment. They can all be accessed on shop.camelfrogpodcast.com. We already have our first listing up which is a long sleeve Series 3 tee. Feel free to check that out!

Also don’t forget! Series 3 is coming in September! Stay tuned, we are almost in to September! All of the staff will be returning in Mid-September to begin full production. Next week, the Senior staff members are going into full-time work. That means our level 2 staff will be on the works. There might be less inquiries until then. For now, Episodes 13 and 14 are up for September and October release. Each episodes comes out at least once a month. Our goal is to have 1 episode up at least once a month. This consistency helps us with productions then the full release.

Alright, that’s gonna wrap up the updates for now! Keep track of our updates on Social Media, and we’ll see you soon for the next one! Take care everyone.
