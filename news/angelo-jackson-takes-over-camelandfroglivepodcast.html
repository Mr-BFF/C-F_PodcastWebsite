Angelo takes over Camel and Frog Podcast!

By AngeloJacksonED - Jan 15th, 2021.


*As you might have known over the course of the last few days, Mr B.F.F the creator of the The Camel and the Frog Live! has chosen to leave the podcast on a good note. According to the information given, we saw Mr B.F.F as a grace of excitement, happiness and has the love for this activity for 5 years. Upon later leaving very soon, he has written this note in his favor. Soon after, Angelo succeeded him to start off again on a good note once more for the podcast. Given by the 4 episodes Mr B.F.F produced, he immediately got stressed out too much, Angelo assisted him in preparing a script when episode 4 came in. Then he modified it to make is as "safe for work" as it should be because he thought the jokes were way too "cheesy". Angelo was soon trained to expect what kind of content is allowed since the distributor who sends out the podcast also had safeguards in place.

You are now looking at these messages; Attached are the two notes regarding the podcast members.

NOTE FROM Mr B.F.F (former director and moderator)

Hi folks. This is the most important announcement to make. I'm also bringing you some big news that's going to benefit the podcast. Back in 2015 when I first started videos on YouTube, I thought The Camel and Frog Live! wasn't going to be successful. As my channel grew during the years with video games which eventually became a stack of other things I would like to host, even with the shout-outs from my friends and supporters. Stress built up and eventually, I can no longer afford working with many stuff I tested and made and this was one of them. So I decided to share via Discord and YouTube and many other socials, but most didn't respond. However, late last month or two, we were making a deal with AngeloJacksonED, who shows love for my series and the podcast. Not only he cared about it, but he was trying to promote and make the podcast better. After hearing his ideas and plans, I think that this was the way to go, and that was the right choice going forward. Angelo and I talked for months and eventually we reached an agreement for a deal to make Angelo the new owner of the Podcast in Early December. His deal was to have 100% of revenue go to him as well as 50% of the tools I make go to him. The reason was I found other ways to monetize my content, like my website and a shared Admob application. Angelo has already begun making some changes, such as bringing friends to assist him. The podcast author will not be changed and Angelo’s aiming toward community growth and gain listeners. I’m can't wait to see how well this goes, and he feels pretty confident too. Attached will be Angelo's introduction to his story for the podcast. The podcast will begin again pretty soon. For me, I will be moving on and leaving the podcast. I might still catch an episode here and there though. Thank you to all! So Long folks! Always remember to stay safe and hit me up whenever you need it! Mr B.F.F#5418 on Discord!

Notice from AngeloJacksonED (current director)

Hello everyone! My name is Angelo Jackson, aka AngeloJacksonED! It's nice to meet you! As you might have known, I've been added as a Moderator, Director and Producer for the podcast, for a great deal because I loved watching Mr B.F.F and his videos so I reached out and agreed on what I said. The plan is to grow a community with our podcast. Just a while ago, Audry.io reached out to us with promotions with our podcast, we'll try and see if we can promote it there. More updates to spare but for now, but the priorities are now set to go, fixing the website and promoting the podcast. I am pretty excited to see how this will go in the long run. :) - AngeloJacksonED.

We wish nothing but Mr B.F.F all the best in the future.
