Frog Reacts? More Ideas are Being Planned!

By AngeloJacksonED - June 12th, 2021.



Reactions, more Podcasts? Let's talk about them Specifically.

Frog reacting to something! Teaser Poster.

Hello. Did you forget to watch Episode 11? Please have a look at it at the listen page or on the Streaming Services! Hope you like the big changes that came in effect including the notable host, reporters and co-hosts! Let us know what you think!

Today, this article is a little different because we are showing plans on how we plan to have Frog in and out of the podcast. More work but more fun is about to be made. Even though we have a central area where you can check for podcast updates like our website apparently, recently, we had a Twitter account for small updates and pictures for trailers, Ko-fi for donations which we might move to another donation host, a typeform for feedback and a merch store, although our merch host keeps rejecting our items for low quality work….. the team was not happy about that….. not only it can make a bad experience, we plan to move to Spring in the future since they have better tools to help us sell as well so we will say bye to Spreadshop. It’s just much better than the other, now back to the plans, we want to talk about, these are the ideas we made relates to Frog and the podcast so let’s get started.

Note that these plans are drafts so don’t get too excited just yet, we need to work more on them in order for us to release it. After the Finale which is episode 12, we can start planning and filming.

So the team planned to have Frog on center stage for probably months since a full video was never released on him….. We are doing a Frog Reacts video and this can probably go on for months and months if we think it’s successful. We do have a YouTube channel for our Podcast videos and other stuff related to the Camel and Frog Live Podcast to go up and be seen.

So what’s the Frog Reacts?

Similar to one of friends reaction videos, we wanted to add Frog and watch him look at a video and react to it rather than sharing an audio file of it. Audio files will be continued to be looked at on the podcast. But still, Frog would be capable of doing the videos and we’ll provide the right reaction based on his personalities. More to come in the mean-time for that. We hope to spend time on it soon.

Second Plan?

The staff have decided to have another podcast from the creators of The Camel and Frog Live! Podcast as they say. EDTeam14 already set an initiative to build more podcast and since the podcast rights belongs to them, staff will also spend time working on another podcast. Maybe we thought that Frog Does It All get a separate Website and Podcast, however, with the on-going situation with Camel and a few Website and Infrastructure costs we need to cover, making a business profile for the podcast must be ditched for the time being. However, you may support our work via Ko-fi. Click here to donate and it helps us support the work. We are planning to move donation hosts even it costs us taxes, we are here to support our community as well. EDTeam14 is planning to have the podcast on the works first before planning financial costs because we don’t want to use lots of money in this case because it is very expensive. Hence the Initiative, they do plan to have 2 or 3 podcasts distributed in the near future. Thanks to Anchor, the podcasts we make are free of charge. Anyways, Good luck with that!

Any third plan?

No. That’s all the podcast can currently supply on. Since the start of Summer gets us busy, we are definitely busy because Episode 12 is set to be released on July which is also the Season Finale. We hope things work out so we can do it and not fail. So make sure you keep in the loop for Episode 12 and watch episode 11!

Don’t forget about the Anchor messages, just submit an audio file regarding your ideas and it might end up being in our future podcast episode! After all of this work, this is making me hungry……

See you all next time.
