About the Staff

LEARN MORE ABOUT US (characters) and our Podcast Mission.


Welcome to Camel & Frog Live Podcast's Website. We log updates and highlights regarding our podcast. Feel free to check back everytime for updates. This site is updated mostly by staff, admins, Angelo and Andrew who are members and proffesionals of our podcast.

Meet Camel

Camel is best known for being the main host of the podcast, he's normally active 99% of time when the podcast strikes every airing date. His first ever apperance is when he was with robot, (which is now deleted), along came with archived videos on top of that that never been released. Today, Camel is a fan of media, host of the Parody WWTBAM and he is named, the special and fluffy pal for the "Camel and Frog Live!" podcast. Although normal Camels drink about 20 gallons of water, he can drink 20 gallons of milk!

4 Words to best describe Camel: Friendly, Caring, delight and and a joy!

Meet Frog

Frog is best known as the co-host of "The Camel and Frog Live! Podcast." Frog didn't debut until Christmas 2017 when we were filming with the rest of the characters in practice films. Frog's friend is named "Butterfly" and their most common trait is the "JoyTV" badge attached to their bodies. Frog succeeded him in 2018. Today, Frog was a contestant in the parody WWTBAM and guest contestant in the parody version of Super Millionaire as well!

Meet the Creator, Mr B.F.F!

Mr B.F.F is the creator of "The Camel and The Frog Live!" and "The Camel And Frog Live Podcast". He served as a Director in 2015-2017, and now he is a Wii Gamer who primarily plays in Wii Sports Golf. Camel made a cameo appearance in one of his recordings in 2016 and 2017. In 2020, he filmed a short video on parodies of WWTBAM which has served quite nicely. He's no longer a director and isn't creating anymore for the The Camel and The Frog Live! as it served downfall through present day. We will continue to honor his name through our podcast as his name is written as the author of the podcast.
